Gunsmith & Shooting Sports


Welcome to Stack & Tier's accessory dealer & gunsmith service serving the pacific northwest.

Services offered include custom pistol and rifle builds, caliberation, maintence, cleaning and restoration; optic mounting and caliberation; basic to intermediate pistol and rifle training.

We source directly from manufacturers, distributors or wholesale dealers. Current builds and a few products are available locally at Sound Loan or directly at Gunbroker.


Building a custom firearm platform that has both original equipment manufacturers and aftermarket manufacturers is both less or as affordable as a complete solution and allows precise customizations. A few manufactures we usually source from include:

    Aero Precision Alpha Shooting Sports KM Tactical Riton Optics Spartan Mounts Stern Defense


Online distributors and wholesalers resources include: * Amazon Business * Gun Accessory Supply , * MGE Wholesale , * RSR Group's ( see inventory , manufacturers , upcoming & new items) * Primary Arms Wholesale.

We will likely be able to beat any online retailer by at least 5%.
