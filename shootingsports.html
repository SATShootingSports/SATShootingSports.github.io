About


Welcome to S&T Shooting Sports. Karl, the owner-operator, is a Washington State Federal Firearms dealer (Type 1), gunsmith and training provider serving the pacific northwest. Karl has a background of working in technical fields involving both mechanical and administrative aptitudes. Our workshop/office is located in Marysville, WA. Equipment available for gunsmith projects is basic; trade specific tools, a few higher-end vices, and a laser engraver (10W Output). We are card carrying members at local ranges, trade organizations, and registered with the largest e-Commerce platforms and retailers i.e. Gunbroker , Brownells , and MidwayUSA

Services


Services offered include firearm transfers (retailer or private party), custom pistol & rifle builds, laser engraving, calibration, maintenance, cleaning and restoration; and optic mounting.

Products we source from include manufacturers, distributors or wholesale volume dealers. Please reference WA Legislature 9.41.090 for delivery and background check waiting periods. More general references regarding federal, state and local laws are available under the "Resources" section on this page.

Selling an firearm (private party transfers); whether it's a pistol, long gun, shotgun, or other seralized item requires the item to be received by an FFL holder, and often depending on the type of firearms and carrier, shipped from an FFL holder. Verify received FFL License with ATF's FFLeZCheck.

Selling a firearm online that usually involves shipping the item can involve risk. If the buyer is unable to take possession after a background check and the FFL Holder is unable to release the firearm. The costs to ship the item back to the seller are then assumed by the buyer, or reverts back to the seller. It is worth any effort to ensure private party transfer agreement terms and the receiving FFL Holder policies. The following are a few resources with more details regarding the private party shipping process including shipping carrier policies (USPS, UPS, & Fedex) Gunbroker , Wa Gun Law
