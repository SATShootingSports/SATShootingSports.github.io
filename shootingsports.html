About


Welcome to Stack & Tier's FFL dealer, gunsmith and training services serving the pacific northwest.
Services offered include custom pistol and rifle build customization, caliberation, maintenance, cleaning and restoration; optic mounting and caliberation.

We source directly from manufacturers, distributors or wholesale volume dealers. Current builds and a few products are available in-store Sound Loan or at Gunbroker. Our e-Commerce website is planned to launch in Fall 2023.
Building a custom firearm platform that has both original equipment manufacturers and aftermarket manufacturers can be both less or as affordable as a complete solution that allows precise customizations. We're happy to assist at any point in a build process including planning, design, estimation, materials procurement.


We also can offer basic pistol and rifle training for shooters of beginner to moderate skill levels from shooters just starting out to volume-reloading. Training includes a range of topics including range and backstop design and construction, reloading, equipment, bullet casting ("hard" and/or powder coated), brass preparation, and firearm accessories.
